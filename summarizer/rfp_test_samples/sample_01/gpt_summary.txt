The RFP is for the construction of a half-size basketball court. The contractor is required to provide and install a new concrete floor slab 120 mm thick, reinforced as per drawings, with an acrylic surface, half-court lines, and coloring. The scope includes excavation, compaction, leveling, formwork, vapor barrier installation, and concrete pouring. The contractor must also provide and install one 72” height adjustable basketball hoop with foundation, anchored to the concrete slab. Optional work includes the installation of a chain-link fence approximately 19.6 meters long and 3.65 meters high with metal supports, doors, and associated materials. Exterior lighting fixtures are also part of the project.

All concrete work must include proper reinforcement, vapor barriers, formwork, and compaction. Acrylic surface coating and court line marking must comply with manufacturer specifications. Any damage during other trades’ work must be repaired. The contractor is responsible for dust control during excavation and must submit product data, cut sheets, samples, and safety data sheets for basketball accessories prior to installation.

Pricing should include division by general requirements, site work, excavation, concrete, reinforcement, final acrylic surface, labor, materials, overhead, profit, and total cost. Optional costs for the chain-link fence and associated materials should be provided separately. The project estimate is anticipated to be between $25,000 and $100,000.
