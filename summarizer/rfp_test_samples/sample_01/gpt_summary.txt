RFP SUMMARY – HALF-SIZE BASKETBALL COURT

Division/Description

Construction of a new half-size outdoor basketball court.

Includes earthwork, concrete slab, reinforcement, acrylic sport surface, line marking, accessories, and one adjustable hoop.

Optional scope: chain-link fence with doors and exterior lighting fixtures.

Concrete slab size: approximately 16.8 m x 15 m x 120 mm thick, reinforced with specified bars and vapor barrier.

Acrylic surface finishing, multi-coat application, cleaning, and restoration of any damage.

Hoop foundation and installation to professional standards; 72" adjustable unit, 10 ft (3.05 m) rim height, tempered-glass backboard.

Contractor responsibilities include dust control, compaction, site examination, compliance with all referenced standards, and submission of material data and submittals.

Labor

Labor required for excavation to specified elevations within ±25 mm tolerance.

Labor for compaction using a roller with minimum three passes; removal and replacement of soft soil.

Labor to install formwork, pour and finish reinforced concrete slab, place vapor barrier, and install footing for hoop.

Labor to apply acrylic coatings, court lines, and perform final touch-ups.

Labor to install optional chain-link fence, supports, and lighting fixtures.

Labor to install basketball post using mechanical anchor bolts and integrate footing pour with slab construction.

Materials

120 mm thick reinforced concrete with specified 8 mm and other indicated reinforcing bars.

6-mil polyethylene vapor barrier.

Acrylic-based surface system, including primers, coatings, and acrylic latex line-marking paint.

One high-quality, adjustable basketball hoop with tempered-glass backboard and mechanical anchoring system.

Optional materials: chain-link fence (approx. 19.6 m long x 3.65 m high), metal supports, gates, painting/finishing materials, and exterior lighting fixtures.

Submittals required for all accessories (catalogs, material data, cut sheets, samples, SDS).

Overhead

Contractor overhead associated with dust-control measures, equipment usage, roller compaction, formwork, safety compliance, and site-specific mobilization.

Overhead for administration, supervision, quality assurance, material handling, and compliance with all FAR clauses, labor standards, and required approvals.

Profit

Contractor profit to be applied per proposal, consistent with the anticipated magnitude of construction (USD 25,000–100,000 range).

Profit factor to account for general construction risk, schedule coordination, material procurement, and optional add-alternates.

Total

Total cost to reflect combined labor, materials, overhead, and profit for the full basketball court scope.

Optional pricing to be listed separately for chain-link fence installation and exterior lighting fixtures.

Overall project estimated within stated magnitude of USD 25,000–100,000 (or equivalent local currency).
